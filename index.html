Welcome To Aristogene Biosciences

Beyond Anti-biotics: Go Antibiotic free

Aristogene Biosciences Pvt. Ltd.

Aristogene Biosciences Pvt. Ltd.

Lead by a team of Women technocrats with a cumulative experience of over 100 years in the field of life sciences, Aristogene has pioneered the development and commercialization of bacteriophage products for treatment of infections in aquaculture and poultry. The need for bacteriophage products is necessitated by the overuse of antibiotics leading to AMR (antimicrobial resistance) problem, a great concern worldwide. Impacted by AMR, many countries have banned the use of antibiotics . Antibiotics are ineffective on bacterial infections and further compounding as no newer antibiotics are in the pipeline. At this crucial juncture, alternatives to antibiotics are the best way forward to make the society healthier. We at Aristogene have developed bacteriophage based products, which are the proven best alternatives to antibiotics.

As a biotech company located in Bangalore, Aristogene is driven by Indian ethos with a global perspective. With over two decades of experience in manufacture of enzymes, biochemicals and molecular biological reagents for research organizations and educational institutions; we have evolved into a global leader in manufacturing of bacteriophage products for treatment of infections in livestock, poultry, aquaculture and agricultural sectors. These products have been extensively tested, tried and are being used in various poultry and aquaculture farms both for prophylaxis and treating infections.

Further, Aristogene is continuing its R&D activities as part of its constant endeavour to contribute in a humble way for a cleaner and sustainable environment.
