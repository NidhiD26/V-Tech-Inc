Elixir

- The anti-vibrio phage formulation

The active ingredients of ELIXIR are naturally occurring bacteriophages that selectively and specifically kill vibrios.

ELIXIR is loaded with high concentrations of potent bacteriophages that can eliminate vibrios from the culture ensuring higher yield and profitability.

Benefits:

Effectively reduces the vibrio counts in ponds and decreases mortality.

No damage to the normal beneficial bacteria.

Active against antibiotic resistant bacteria.

Reduces white gut disease.

Reduces luminous bacteria.

Reduces running mortality.

Green safe naturally alternative to antibiotics.

Easy field application.
