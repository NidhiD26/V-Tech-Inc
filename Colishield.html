Colishield®

- Phage Cocktail for Pathogenic E.coli

Bacteriophages are natural antimicrobial agents that fight against antibiotic resistant bacteria which cause infections in birds, animals, humans or in crops of agricultural value. Phage technology is based on the principle that naturally occurring bacteriophages provide the safest and targeted approach to Combat Bacterial Infections.

Product:

Colishield® is specially formulated cocktail of bacteriophages that can selectively kill pathogenic E.coli. The most effective phages have been carefully selected from the phage library and combined to provide complete protection from E.coli infection;

"Best alternative to antibiotics"

Product Safety:

Overdose of >50 times the normal supplement has not shown any adverse effect on the chicken.

Continuous use of colishield for 100 days on daily basis did not show adverse effect.

Field studies on broilers, breeders and layers showed promising results both with prophylactic and therapeutic treatment.
